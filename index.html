ℹ️ App Information

🎤 Leslie Speech Assistant
Helping people with speech impairments be better understood
🎯 Main Features
• Speech Recognition - Listens to your speech and displays it clearly
• Speech Playback - Speaks your words back clearly for others to hear
• Text Input - Type messages that can be spoken aloud
• Contact Calling - Easy access to your phone contacts
🛡️ Privacy & Safety
• No background listening - microphone only active when you choose
• No data sent to external servers (except optional OpenAI for enhanced recognition)
• All processing happens on your device
• Your contacts and speech data stay private
🔧 How to Use
• Speech Recognition: Click "START TALKING" and speak clearly
• Text Input: Type your message and click "SPEAK" to have it read aloud
• Contacts: Load your real contacts or use the demo ones
• Settings: Adjust speech speed and other preferences
💡 Tips for Best Results
• Speak clearly and at normal volume
• Use the app in a quiet environment when possible
• Adjust speech speed in settings if needed
• Try the text input feature for important messages
